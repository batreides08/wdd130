Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a small Central American country known for its rich biodiversity, beautiful beaches, and strong commitment to environmental conservation. It is bordered by Nicaragua to the north, Panama to the south, the Pacific Ocean to the west, and the Caribbean Sea to the east. The country has a stable democracy, no standing army, and a strong focus on sustainability and ecotourism.
