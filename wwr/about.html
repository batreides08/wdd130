History

Splash White Water Rafting was founded in 2010 by a group of adventure-loving friends who shared a passion for the great outdoors and the thrill of white water. What started as weekend trips on local rivers quickly turned into a full-fledged rafting company dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for people of all ages.

Over the years, Splash has grown from a single raft and a dream into one of the most trusted names in outdoor adventure. With certified guides, top-of-the-line equipment, and a deep respect for nature, our mission is to help people connect with the wild in a fun, safe, and memorable way.
